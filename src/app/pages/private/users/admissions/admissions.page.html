Admissions procedure

At Oxford Prime Academy, we are not academically selective. However, we do believe that our school has a special set of values and want to make sure that a potential pupil will fit the environment we have.

You can begin an application for your child by visiting an open morning in the school, filling in our registration form or just by contacting us at +38 044 334 32 14 or via email at admissions@oxfordprime.com.

Open Mornings

You can start your acquaintance with the school during a pre-appointed open morning. We will be happy to answer any of your questions during the visit.

During the Open Morning, there will be an introduction from the Headmaster or one of our teachers. You will see a normal school day and be able to get a good impression of what life is like at Oxford Prime Academy.

Please fill in the form here to register.

Registration Form

A parent may register their child at Oxford Prime Academy by completing the registration form here. We have up to 6 places in each class with the entry procedure outlined below depending on a particular year group.

You will be contacted by a member of our staff and invited to discover the school, where we will be able to interact with your child, according to his or her age group.

Entry at 4+

After registration, we invite your child to spend some time at Oxford Prime Academy. It is a good opportunity for children to get to know the school and to experience school life. They will try different activities, which may include art, music, sport, science or an academic lesson. They will also play some games.

Subject to our observations made during the visit, children will be offered places at Oxford Prime Academy.
