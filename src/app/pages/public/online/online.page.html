In the exceptional circumstances of quarantine, Oxford Prime Academy is available at home.

As far as possible, we intend to deliver the same education that pupils would otherwise have in the school, including not only academic lessons but also a range of co-curricular activities including sports, the creative arts, drama, music etc.

Continuing to study from home comes with a number of new challenges, however, it also brings opportunities, a new study and rest balance and a different type of focus, - and our online learning style aims to exploit them fully.

In short, Oxford Prime Academy is fully prepared to deliver education both in the school and online.
